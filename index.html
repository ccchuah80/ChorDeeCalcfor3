📜 Rules & Calculation

1. Basic Scoring (基本算法）

Winner collects points from all losers. The basic calculation is:
(Cards × Weight × Multiplier) + BASE Amount

If 【底注也炒】Include BASE in Multiplier is checked
(Cards × Weight + BASE Amount) × Multiplier

2. Multipliers （炒牌）

  • Double (x2): If a player has or more cards. (current setting)
  • Triple (x3): If a player has or more cards.(current setting)
  • Quadruple (x4): If a player has cards. (Not Applicable if >13).
  • Win with ♦️3 (拉尾): If the winner ends the game with the ♦️3, the final payout for that round is doubled for all players.

3. Side Bets (No Side Bet)

If "No Side Bet" is unchecked, losers pay each other based on their card differences. The calculation depends on the Mult. First setting:

Standard (Mult. Last) Friendly
|Diff| × Weight × Max(Mult)

Example (9 vs 12 cards, Weight 1):

1. Diff: |12 - 9| = 3

2. Max Multiplier: x3 (from 12 cards)

Result: 3 × 1 × 3 = 9 pts

Aggressive (Mult. First) High Stakes
|(C1 × M1) - (C2 × M2)| × Weight

Example (9 vs 12 cards, Weight 1):

1. Player 1: 9 cards × 2 (x2) = 18

2. Player 2: 12 cards × 3 (x3) = 36

Result: |36 - 18| × 1 = 18 pts

4. Bonus (Special Hands)

Use the "Bonus" button for Straight Flush or 4 of a Kind. Values entered here are added directly to the total. Note: The sum of all bonuses inputs must equal 0.
